M E D I A R E L E A S E

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE October 10, 2010 New York Comic Con, New York, NY NEW YORK HOSTS THE LAUNCH FOR AUSTRALIA BASED GLOBAL FILM & TELEVISION AWARDS SHOW Australian company 101010 Productions launch The Apollo Awards for Science Fiction and Fantasy Film and Television at New York Comic Con. 101010 Productions have partnered with Australias award winning production and web entertainment companies TPD Media and Zone4 to introduce a worldwide Film and Television award ceremony unique to the internet. Battlestar Galactica alumnus, actor Richard Hatch is assisting with the promotion of the media launch of The Apollo Awards at New York Comic Con at the Jacob K. Javits Center, taking place on October 10. This date in binary code, 101010, equals the number 42, known throughout the genre as the meaning of life, the universe and everything from the international best seller The Hitch Hikers Guide to the Galaxy by Douglas Adams. At New York Comic Con, The Apollo Awards team make their official public announcement of the event, and unveil the trophy design and major details of the 2011 awards ceremony. The Apollo Awards are so named for the Greek and Roman god Apollo. Son of Zeus, Apollo was one of the most significant and influential gods of ancient Greek and Roman mythology. Over the decades, Apollo has appeared in science fiction and fantasy film and television both as the God and also by name, in series such as Star Trek, Battlestar Galactica, Stargate, to cult fantasy adventure shows Xena: Warrior Princess and Hercules: The Legendary Journeys. The realms of mythology, science and science fiction merged when the first NASA moon landing missions were codenamed 'Apollo'. In the many Film and Television awards given out annually, science fiction and fantasy has never experienced the recognition they deserve, say the creators of the Apollo Awards. Peter Budd, Director of 101010 Productions said, What makes The Apollo Awards unique is that the choice is truly and firmly in the hands of the worldwide audience. From the Americas and Europe to Africa, Asia and Oceania, both nominations and votes are in the hands of the people. For this global alliance of fans, The Apollo Awards will be delivered online, live and worldwide, rather than through broadcast or cable TV. Co-creator of the Apollo Awards, Melanie Teychenne-King says, The internet is the future of television, of breaking down the barriers between regions and time zones. By streaming the award ceremony live across the globe were giving everyone the chance to participate on an even level, to know they have ownership of the show. The purpose of 101010 Productions, owners and creators of The Apollo Awards, is to unite the worldwide community of science fiction and fantasy audiences and in doing so, create opportunities for the global creative community in developing and producing projects in these genres. The first teaser trailer for the Apollo Awards is available on YouTube at http://www.youtube.com/TheApolloAwards Information regarding international business alliances and sponsors will be forthcoming. No further public announcements will be made until November 23rd - the birth date of Boris Karloff, made famous by his roles in the 1930s Frankenstein films. Media enquiries only can be directed to media@101010productions.com *** INFORMATION AND BIOGRAPHIES THE APOLLO AWARDS WEBCAST The Apollo Awards Ceremony is a gala red-carpet event to be staged in Queensland, Australia in mid-2011. Utilising state-of-the-art integration technologies, live presentations and performances will be combined with pre-produced entertainment segments and acceptances. The Apollo Awards will employ the classic award categories of Best Actor, Best Film, and Best Series etcetera, as well as irreverent categories. NEW YORK COMIC CON The New York Comic Con is an annual New York City convention dedicated to comics, graphic novels, anime, video games, toys, movies, and television. Now in its fifth year, The New York Comic Con is a for-profit event produced and managed by ReedPOP, a division of Reed Business. RICHARD HATCH Best known for his role as Apollo in the classic 1978 Battlestar Galactica and as Tom Zarek in the 2004 Syfy re-imagining of the same series, Richard is an accomplished author, director and producer, as well as an acting and life coach. He is currently co-creating and starring several web series (WhoTheFrak.com and The Hunted: Vindication). 101010 PRODUCTIONS Peter Budd and Melanie Teychenne-King, Directors of 101010 Productions Pty. Ltd. and independent Event Managers, founded the company to specialise in the creation and delivery of unique, world-class events for the entertainment media in Science Fiction and Fantasy sector. Both have worked on numerous large scale entertainment events around Australia, coordinating logistics and operations, as well as managing notable actors from series and films such as the Star Trek and Stargate franchises, Doctor Who, Buffy The Vampire Slayer, Star Wars, Lord of the Rings and the Harry Potter franchise. Senior Development Manager of 101010 Productions, David Franken is a former 25 year programmer at the Seven Network Australia, a lifelong science fiction and fantasy enthusiast and currently a television production consultant and screen writer. T.P.D. MEDIA TPD Media has the largest media output of the state of Queensland, Australia. The company offers a synergy of technology and talent through its in-house, high definition technology, award-winning crews, and a team of video-graphic designers working in 2D and 3D animation. ZONE 4 ZONE4 is a Digital Content Company, founded by EMMY and BAFTA award winning Thom Saunders. It has successfully delivered over 700 projects across multiple digital platforms, including the global campaign for the TV show LOST with ABC America. ### ENDS
